About Me

Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Spencer,

I'm a Project and Event Management Professional currently based in Santa Cruz, CA. I plan engaging community events, lead successful fundraising campaigns, and implement new programs that boost organizational capacity.

My professional work thus far has been focused in the non-profit sector. I am passionate about solving complex social issues and serving as a leader for change. Over the last three years, I have:

  • Raised over $1,000,000 for more than 150 local organizations.
  • Recruited and supervised hundreds of community volunteers through both existing and newly created volunteer programs
  • Trained non-profit professionals in a variety of subjects including leadership, event planning, and fundraising

In addition to non-profit work, my educational background is focused in health and wellness. I earned my Bachelor of Science degree in Kinesiology from Cal Poly San Luis Obispo, and enjoy using my understanding of exercise and diet to lead a healthy life.

Beyond these focus areas, I also have a strong interest in web development, digital marketing, and photography. I believe in constant learning, and enjoy using my free time to expand my repertoire of expertise in these areas.

Want to learn more about my events, projects, photos, or web work? Check out my portfolio here.

In my free time, you’ll find me out surfing, playing my guitar, or doodling in my sketchbook. I love sitting outside on misty mornings with my coffee and a good book or relaxing around a campfire with friends. Always looking to learn new things, I enjoy the opportunity to explore new skills, ideas, and places. Eventually, I’d like to make my way to all 7 continents, and one day all the way up to the moon (one can dream right?). Moving forward, I am excited to continue to pursue my passions and give back wherever I can.

So, do you want to make something happen? If so, I’d love to help. Please feel free to write me a message at spencer.takata@gmail.com
