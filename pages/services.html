Project Management


As a Project Manager, I’m committed to achieving real results for my clients on time and on budget. My approach to project management is centered on three main pillars:

  1. Support and empower individual project team members, arming them with the resources, clarity, and self-efficacy to achieve project deliverables.
  2. Understand and drive all stakeholder priorities, ensuring project requirements are realized with nothing but the highest quality.
  3. Proactively identify opportunities for efficiencies, process improvements, and project development, facilitating continuous growth.

With this philosophy, I’ve led digital marketing projects, agency creative work, award ceremonies, fundraisers, multi-faceted volunteer initiatives, and more.

Web Development


Want to bring your brand into the digital space? Or maybe give your existing site a fresh new look? Either way, I'm here to help.

I build sleek, engaging, mobile responsive websites for small businesses, nonprofits, and individuals who want to stand out from the crowd. Whether you’re starting from scratch or coming in for a tune up, I will take a personalized, goal oriented approach to optimizing your online presence.

On top of building the nuts and bolts of the site, I’m happy to help with other aspects of building your brand including:

  • Designing a look and feel for your site
  • Sourcing beautiful, professional imagery
  • Writing keyword rich web copy
  • Finding your brand’s voice
  • Developing a logo

Together, we can talk through your aspirations and ideas to make sure your site truly representantes your brand and supports your goals.
